User Panel
	1. Profile image updation of self.
	2. Mark attendance with photo (Check-in, Check-out)
	3. Apply for leave, view leave balance and leave history
	4. Submit attendance correction requests
Admin Panel
	1. Dashboard (Total employees, On-time today, Late today, On-time percentage)
	2. Approve/reject leave requests
	3. Review and act on attendance correction requests\
	4. View full list of users
	5. Generate attendance reports (filter by date range, user, in-status, out-status)
	6. Generate leave reports (filter by date range, user, status)
	7. Update profile image of self
